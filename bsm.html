  1. Banana Smoothie

  2. Ingredients:

    • Ripe bananas
    • 1 cup yogurt
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 1-2 tablespoons honey

  3. Procedure:

    • Place sliced bananas in blender, add yogurt, milk, honey into the blender
    • Blend all the ingredients on high speed until the mixture becomes smooth and creamy. This usually takes about 30 seconds to 1 minute,.
