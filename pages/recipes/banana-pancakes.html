  1. Banana Pancakes

  2. Ingredients:

    • 2 ripe bananas
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 cup milk
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 tablespoon butter (for cooking)
    • Optional: honey or maple syrup for serving

  3. Procedure:

    • In a large bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth
    • Add eggs to the mashed bananas and whisk together until well combined
    • Add flour, baking powder, milk, and vanilla extract to the mixture
    • Stir all ingredients until you have a smooth batter (don't overmix)
    • Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat and add a small amount of butter
    • Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake
    • Cook for 2-3 minutes until bubbles form on the surface, then flip
    • Cook for another 2 minutes until golden brown on both sides
    • Serve warm with honey, maple syrup, or fresh fruit

    Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 4

    Cost: Under $5
