  1. Banana Smoothie

  2. Ingredients:

    • 2 ripe bananas (fresh or frozen)
    • 1 cup plain or vanilla yogurt
    • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
    • 1-2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 4-5 ice cubes (if using fresh bananas)
    • Optional: 1 tablespoon peanut butter or a handful of spinach

  3. Procedure:

    • Peel and slice the bananas into chunks
    • Place sliced bananas in the blender
    • Add yogurt, milk, honey, and vanilla extract into the blender
    • If using fresh bananas, add ice cubes for a cold, thick smoothie
    • Blend all ingredients on high speed until smooth and creamy (30 seconds to 1 minute)
    • Taste and adjust sweetness by adding more honey if needed
    • Pour into glasses and serve immediately
    • Optional: Top with sliced banana, granola, or chia seeds

    Prep Time: 5 minutes | Serves: 2

    Cost: Under $2
