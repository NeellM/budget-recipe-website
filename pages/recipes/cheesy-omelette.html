  1. Cheesy Omelette

  2. Ingredients:

    • 3 large eggs
    • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
    • 2 tablespoons milk
    • 1 tablespoon butter
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Optional fillings: diced bell peppers, mushrooms, spinach, tomatoes, or ham
    • Fresh herbs (optional): chives or parsley

  3. Procedure:

    • Crack the eggs into a bowl and add milk, salt, and pepper
    • Whisk the eggs vigorously until well combined and slightly frothy
    • If using vegetables, dice them into small pieces
    • Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter
    • Once butter is melted and sizzling, pour in the egg mixture
    • Let the eggs cook undisturbed for 30 seconds until edges start to set
    • Using a spatula, gently push the edges toward the center, tilting the pan to let uncooked egg flow to the edges
    • When eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny on top, add cheese and any fillings to one half
    • Fold the omelette in half using a spatula
    • Cook for another 30 seconds, then slide onto a plate
    • Garnish with fresh herbs if desired and serve hot

    Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 5 minutes | Serves: 1

    Cost: Under $2
