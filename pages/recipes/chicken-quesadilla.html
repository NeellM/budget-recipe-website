  1. Chicken Quesadilla

  2. Ingredients:

    • 4 flour tortillas
    • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
    • 1 cup shredded cheese
    • 1/2 bell pepper, diced
    • 1/4 onion, diced
    • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • Salsa and sour cream for serving

  3. Procedure:

    • Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions and peppers until soft
    • Add shredded chicken and warm through
    • Place tortilla in pan, sprinkle half with cheese
    • Add chicken mixture on top of cheese
    • Add more cheese and fold tortilla in half
    • Cook 2-3 minutes per side until golden and cheese melts
    • Cut into wedges and serve with salsa and sour cream

    Prep: 10 min | Cook: 15 min | Serves: 4 | Cost: Under $7
