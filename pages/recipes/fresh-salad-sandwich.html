  1. Fresh Salad Sandwich

  2. Ingredients:

    • 4 slices whole wheat bread
    • 1 cup mixed lettuce leaves
    • 1 tomato, sliced
    • 1/2 cucumber, thinly sliced
    • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
    • 1/2 bell pepper, sliced
    • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise or hummus
    • 1 tablespoon mustard (optional)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Optional: sliced cheese, avocado, or sprouts

  3. Procedure:

    • Wash and prepare all vegetables - slice tomato, cucumber, bell pepper, and onion
    • Toast the bread slices lightly if desired for extra crunch
    • Spread mayonnaise or hummus on one side of each bread slice
    • If using mustard, spread it on the other slices
    • Layer lettuce leaves on the bottom slice of bread
    • Add tomato slices, cucumber, bell pepper, and onion on top
    • Season with a pinch of salt and pepper
    • Add cheese or avocado if using
    • Top with the second slice of bread, spread side down
    • Cut diagonally for easier eating and serve fresh

    Prep Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2

    Cost: Under $5
