  1. Fruit Salad

  2. Ingredients:

    • 2 apples, diced
    • 2 oranges, peeled and segmented
    • 1 cup grapes (red or green), halved
    • 1 banana, sliced
    • 1 cup strawberries, hulled and halved
    • 1/2 cup blueberries
    • 2 tablespoons honey or agave syrup
    • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
    • Optional: fresh mint leaves for garnish

  3. Procedure:

    • Wash all fruits thoroughly under cold water
    • Prepare the fruits: dice apples, peel and segment oranges, halve grapes and strawberries
    • Slice the banana just before serving to prevent browning
    • In a large bowl, combine all the prepared fruits
    • In a small bowl, whisk together honey and lemon juice
    • Pour the honey-lemon dressing over the fruit
    • Gently toss all fruits together to coat with the dressing
    • Chill in the refrigerator for 15-30 minutes before serving
    • Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired
    • Serve cold as a healthy breakfast or snack

    Prep Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 6

    Cost: Under $7
