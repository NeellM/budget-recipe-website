  1. Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  2. Ingredients:

    • 4 slices bread
    • 4 slices cheddar cheese (or your favorite cheese)
    • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
    • Optional: 2 slices tomato or ham

  3. Procedure:

    • Butter one side of each bread slice
    • Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat
    • Place one slice of bread butter-side down in the pan
    • Layer cheese slices on top (add tomato or ham if using)
    • Top with second bread slice, butter-side up
    • Cook for 2-3 minutes until bottom is golden brown
    • Carefully flip sandwich and cook other side for 2-3 minutes
    • Press down gently with spatula for even browning
    • Remove when both sides are golden and cheese is melted
    • Cut diagonally and serve hot

    Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 6 minutes | Serves: 2

    Cost: Under $2
