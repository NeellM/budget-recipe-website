  1. Healthy Oatmeal

  2. Ingredients:

    • 1 cup rolled oats
    • 2 cups water or milk
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1 tablespoon honey or brown sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
    • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Toppings: fresh berries, sliced banana, nuts, or dried fruit
    • Optional: chia seeds, flax seeds, or peanut butter

  3. Procedure:

    • In a medium saucepan, bring water or milk to a boil
    • Add salt and reduce heat to medium-low
    • Stir in the rolled oats and cook, stirring occasionally
    • Simmer for 5-7 minutes until oats are soft and have absorbed most of the liquid
    • Remove from heat and stir in honey, cinnamon, and vanilla extract
    • Let stand for 2-3 minutes to thicken
    • Transfer to serving bowls
    • Add your favorite toppings: fresh berries, sliced bananas, nuts, or dried fruit
    • For extra nutrition, sprinkle with chia seeds or flax seeds
    • Serve warm and enjoy immediately

    Prep Time: 2 minutes | Cook Time: 7 minutes | Serves: 2

    Cost: Under $2
