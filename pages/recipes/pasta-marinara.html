  1. Simple Pasta Marinara

  2. Ingredients:

    • 8 oz spaghetti or penne pasta
    • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Parmesan cheese for serving
    • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

  3. Procedure:

    • Bring a large pot of salted water to boil
    • Cook pasta according to package directions, drain and set aside
    • In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat
    • Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1 minute until fragrant
    • Pour in crushed tomatoes and stir well
    • Add basil, oregano, salt, and pepper
    • Simmer sauce for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally
    • Toss cooked pasta with the sauce
    • Serve hot with grated Parmesan and fresh basil

    Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Serves: 4

    Cost: Under $5
