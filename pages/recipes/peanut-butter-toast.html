  1. Peanut Butter Toast

  2. Ingredients:

    • 2 slices whole wheat bread
    • 3 tablespoons peanut butter
    • 1 banana, sliced
    • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
    • Pinch of cinnamon
    • Optional: chia seeds or granola for topping

  3. Procedure:

    • Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy
    • Spread peanut butter generously on each slice while still warm
    • Arrange banana slices on top of the peanut butter
    • Drizzle with honey if desired
    • Sprinkle with a pinch of cinnamon
    • Add chia seeds or granola for extra crunch
    • Serve immediately while warm

    Prep Time: 5 minutes | Serves: 2

    Cost: Under $2
