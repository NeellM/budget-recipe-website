  1. Veggie Wrap

  2. Ingredients:

    • 2 large whole wheat tortillas
    • 1/4 cup hummus
    • 1 cup mixed lettuce leaves
    • 1/2 cucumber, julienned
    • 1 carrot, grated
    • 1/2 bell pepper, sliced
    • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
    • 2 tablespoons feta cheese (optional)
    • Salt and pepper to taste

  3. Procedure:

    • Warm tortillas in a pan or microwave for 15 seconds
    • Spread hummus evenly over each tortilla
    • Layer lettuce leaves in the center
    • Add cucumber, carrot, bell pepper, and onion on top
    • Sprinkle with feta cheese if using
    • Season with salt and pepper
    • Fold in the sides and roll tightly from bottom to top
    • Cut in half diagonally and serve

    Prep Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2

    Cost: Under $5
