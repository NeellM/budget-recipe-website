  1. Yogurt Parfait

  2. Ingredients:

    • 2 cups vanilla or plain Greek yogurt
    • 1 cup granola
    • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
    • 1 banana, sliced
    • 2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup
    • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds or walnuts)
    • Optional: chia seeds, coconut flakes, or dark chocolate chips

  3. Procedure:

    • Wash and prepare all fruits - hull and slice strawberries if needed
    • If using plain yogurt, mix in honey or maple syrup to sweeten
    • Get two clear glasses or bowls for layering
    • Start with a layer of yogurt at the bottom (about 1/4 cup)
    • Add a layer of granola on top of the yogurt
    • Add a layer of mixed berries and banana slices
    • Repeat the layers: yogurt, granola, fruit
    • Continue layering until glasses are filled, finishing with fruit on top
    • Sprinkle with chopped nuts and optional toppings like chia seeds or coconut flakes
    • Drizzle with a little extra honey if desired
    • Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 2 hours before serving

    Prep Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2

    Cost: Under $5
